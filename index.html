Legal and legal-adjacent projects in the broader fediverse.

The fediverse is a decentralized network of social platforms that use common protocols to communicate with each other. Users across different platforms can interact, fostering a sense of community and collaboration.
Lawstodon.org
The white pages for lawyers and jurists in the fediverse.
esq.social
The largest legal and law-adjacent Mastodon instance in the fediverse.
esq.social Matrix
A Matrix server for legal professionals and academics. The server for third-party connecting purposes is here and the main room is #lawgeneral.
About
These projects are lovingly (mostly) created and/or maintained by Andrew Leahey. They are entirely ad-free, and while labor is donated, the costs of maintenance may be defrayed by people donating to the esq.social Patreon.
