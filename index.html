lawfedi.org

Legal and legal-adjacent projects in the broader fediverse.

Lawstodon.org - the white pages for lawyers and jurists in the fediverse. esq.social - the largest legal and law-adjacent Mastodon instance in the fediverse. esq.social Matrix - a Matrix server for legal professionals and academics. Server: https://matrix.matrix.esq.social, main room: #lawgeneral.
